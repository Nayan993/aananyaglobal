Prabhakar Singh

Profile & Qualifications

Born at Prayagraj on 1st August 1958 in the family of Freedom Fighters namely Late Shri Kashinath Singh (younger brother of my grandfather Late Shri Gaya Prasad Singh) of village Deodih of Ballia district in U.P. The Government has recognized the sacrifice made by the family during the Freedom Struggle after Independence.

Possess specialized skills in:

  • Got selected in prestigious I.I.T at Kharagpur and did B.Tech with Honours in Mechanical Engineering. (1974 -79)
  • MBA in Enterprise Management through the National Management Programme (NMP) from MDI Gurugram.
  • Special Post Graduate Diploma in Environmental Management from IHS Rotterdam, the Netherlands.
  • Low-Cost Housing course from National Housing Authority, Bangkok, Thailand.

Work Experience

  • Worked for Indo-Swiss Energy Co-operation in Switzerland.

Worked for labour reforms and upliftment of the unorganized backward and underprivileged classes.
