Veenay Kumar

Profile

I was born in Konda village of district Bastar (then Madhya Pradesh) and received my primary education in Shri Ramakrishna Mission School. In my adolescence, I joined Swami Vivekananda Foundation, Belur Math and during this time, at the young age of 6, I got engaged in various social works for the upliftment of the local tribal society. Got the highest position in class five in the then Madhya Pradesh Education Board. I received my secondary and higher education from Lucknow, the capital of Uttar Pradesh, where I was considered a bright student with deep social commitment.
Work Experience:
- Year 1991 as independent India reporter
- Rashtriya Sahara, Hindi daily news from the year 1997-2010
- Experience of working as a reporter/senior advertising officer in Sahara Samay News Channel from the year 2010-2014.
- Air Sahara Airlines as Business Development/MCO for a period of 1 year
- Sahara Care/Global for corporate business development
- Project of Railways and Agriculture on Smart Card System for SaharaNext
- During the Corona epidemic, I got the experience of working with the Department of Financial Services (under the Ministry of Finance) of the Government of India in the Self-reliant India campaign of the respected illustrious Prime Minister Shri Narendra Bhai Modi and the suggestions given by me for the MSME sector were accepted by the department.
- Under the auspices of Rashtriya Seva Bharti, participated as a special invitee in the Seva Sangam program organized in Jaipur in April 2023, where I received the company and blessings of the most respected Sarsanghchalak, respected Shri Mohan Bhagwat ji.
Core Competency:
1. Higher Education 5. Food and Public Distribution
2. Railways, Health and Welfare 6. Journalism, Telecommunication
3. Co-operation and Marketing 7. Flying and Civil Aviation
4. Small and Medium Enterprises 8. Information Technology and Service Sector
Positions held:

- Editor in Chief of the famous monthly magazine
- Former Governing Body Member, Bharti College (Delhi University)
- Former IQAC Member Baba Saheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Central University (Lucknow)

Awards:

- Awadh Smriti Samman 2022, by Shri Satyadev Pachauri ji MP Kanpur and Shri Rakesh Sachan (MSME Minister Uttar Pradesh Government).
Technical Skills:
